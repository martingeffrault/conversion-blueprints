# Local Business Website Recipe

> **Business Type**: Service-based local businesses (plumbers, lawyers, dentists, restaurants, salons, etc.)
> **Primary Goals**: Generate local leads, phone calls, appointments, foot traffic
> **Revenue Model**: Service fees, appointments, in-store sales
> **Target**: Geographic area (city, region, neighborhood)

---

## The Local Search Reality

> "Over 60% of local searches now happen on mobile devices. Users expect instant answers, click-to-call functionality, and seamless directions to your business."
>
> *Source: [Growth Minded Marketing](https://growthmindedmarketing.com/blog/local-seo-guide/)*

Local business websites must prioritize:
1. **Immediate contact options** (click-to-call, directions)
2. **Trust signals** (reviews, credentials, years in business)
3. **Local relevance** (location pages, service areas)
4. **Mobile experience** (most local searches are mobile)

1. **What you do** (service type)
2. **Where you do it** (location/area)
3. **How to contact** (phone, book, directions)

## Local SEO Essentials

### On-Page SEO

> "You need to structure your site in an SEO-friendly way, including correct meta descriptions, title tags, and subheadings."
>
> *Source: [Crowdspring](https://www.crowdspring.com/blog/local-seo/)*

**Page Title Formula**:
```
[Service] in [City], [State] | [Business Name]
Example: "Plumber in Austin, TX | Joe's Plumbing"
```

**Meta Description Formula**:
```
[Service] services in [City]. [Key benefit]. [Trust signal]. Call [phone] for [offer].
Example: "Professional plumbing services in Austin. 24/7 emergency service available. Licensed & insured. Call (555) 123-4567 for a free estimate."
```

### Schema Markup

```json
{
  "@type": "LocalBusiness",
  "name": "Business Name",
  "address": {
    "@type": "PostalAddress",
    "streetAddress": "123 Main St",
    "addressLocality": "Austin",
    "addressRegion": "TX",
    "postalCode": "78701"
  },
  "telephone": "(555) 123-4567",
  "openingHours": "Mo-Fr 08:00-18:00",
  "geo": {
    "@type": "GeoCoordinates",
    "latitude": 30.2672,
    "longitude": -97.7431
  },
  "aggregateRating": {
    "@type": "AggregateRating",
    "ratingValue": "4.9",
    "reviewCount": "200"
  }
}
```

### NAP Consistency

> "Maintaining consistent NAP (Name, Address, and Phone Number) information across your website, social media profiles, and online directories is crucial for local SEO."
>
> *Source: [Collaborada](https://www.collaborada.com/blog/local-seo-small-business-tips)*

Ensure identical NAP on:
- Website (header, footer, contact page)
- Google Business Profile
- Facebook, Yelp, Yellow Pages
- Industry directories
- Local business listings
