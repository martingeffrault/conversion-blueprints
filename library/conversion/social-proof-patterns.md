## Types of Social Proof

### 1. Customer Testimonials

Most powerful form of social proof.

**Effectiveness hierarchy**:
| Type | Trust Level | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Video testimonial | Highest | Real person, authentic |
| Photo + name + title | High | Verifiable identity |
| Name + company | Medium | Some credibility |
| Name only | Low | Could be fake |
| Anonymous | Lowest | Zero credibility |

### 2. Case Studies

**Elements that convert**:
| Element | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Specific numbers/results | +42% engagement |
| Before/after comparison | +35% credibility |
| Named customer + logo | +28% trust |
| Industry relevance | +25% consideration |

### 3. Reviews & Ratings

**Optimal rating display**:
| Rating | Purchase Likelihood |
|--------|---------------------|
| 4.2 - 4.5 stars | Highest |
| 5.0 stars | Lower (seems fake) |
| Below 4.0 | Significant drop |

*Source: Northwestern Spiegel Research Center*

### 4. Logo Clouds

**Best practices**:
- 5-8 logos (more = cluttered)
- Include recognizable brands
- Grayscale for consistency
- Color on hover (optional)
- "Trusted by" headline

### 5. Statistics/Numbers

**Examples**:
- "10,000+ customers"
- "50M+ processed"
- "$1B+ in revenue managed"
- "99.9% uptime"

### 6. Media Mentions

**Format**:
```
"As featured in"
[Forbes] [TechCrunch] [WSJ] [Inc.]
```

### 7. Awards & Certifications

**Examples**:
- "G2 Leader 2025"
- "Product Hunt #1"
- "ISO 27001 Certified"
- "SOC 2 Type II"

## Writing Effective Testimonials

### What Makes a Great Testimonial

| Element | Why It Works |
|---------|--------------|
| Specific outcome | "Increased by 40%" > "Improved a lot" |
| Before/after | Shows transformation |
| Relatable problem | Reader identifies |
| Emotional language | Creates connection |
| Name + photo + title | Credibility |

### Testimonial Request Formula

Ask customers:
1. What problem were you trying to solve?
2. What made you choose us?
3. What specific results have you seen?
4. Would you recommend us? Why?

### Edit for Impact

| Original | Edited for Impact |
|----------|-------------------|
| "Great product, very helpful" | "Cut our support tickets by 60% in 2 months" |
| "Easy to use" | "Onboarded our whole team in one day â€” zero training needed" |
| "Good value" | "ROI paid for itself in the first week" |

| Content | Quote only vs. stats + quote |
| Visual | Photo vs. logo vs. neither |

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

| Anti-Pattern | Problem | Better Approach |
|--------------|---------|-----------------|
| Anonymous quotes | Zero credibility | Full attribution |
| Generic praise | Not convincing | Specific outcomes |
| Stock photos | Obviously fake | Real customer photos |
| Too many testimonials | Overwhelming | Curate best 6-9 |
| No variety | Same industry/role | Mix of perspectives |
| Outdated | "2018" looks stale | Recent testimonials |
| Fake reviews | Legal risk, backfires | Real customers only |
| Only 5-star | Seems fake | Include 4-star |
