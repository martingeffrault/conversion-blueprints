## Platform-Specific Patterns

### Substack Best Practices

- Two-tier pricing (free + paid)
- Annual with 14-day trial
- Monthly: $9-12, Annual: $90-110
- Free posts weekly, paid posts 2-4x/month
- Discussion threads for engagement

### Patreon Best Practices

- 3-5 tiers maximum
- $1 tier for access (builds base)
- $5-10 tier for core content
- $25+ tier for 1:1 or premium
- Monthly exclusive content drops

### Gumroad/Digital Products

- One-time purchases or subscriptions
- Tiered packages (basic/pro/ultimate)
- Bundle discounts
- Affiliate program for creators
- Pay-what-you-want option
