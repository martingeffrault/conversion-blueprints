# Industry Patterns Library

> **Purpose**: Industry-specific design and conversion patterns that work
> **Usage**: Reference when building sites for specific verticals
> **Principle**: Every industry has proven patterns — learn from leaders in each space

---

## Available Guides

### [E-Commerce](./ecommerce.md)
Online retail patterns from Amazon, Shopify stores, fashion retailers, and DTC brands. Product pages, cart optimization, checkout flows, and mobile commerce.

### [Healthcare & Medical](./healthcare.md)
Patterns from hospital systems, telehealth platforms, medical practices, and health tech. Trust signals, HIPAA considerations, appointment booking, and patient portals.

### [Real Estate](./real-estate.md)
Property listing sites, agent portfolios, and real estate platforms. Property search, gallery optimization, lead capture, and map integrations.

### [Restaurants & Hospitality](./restaurants.md)
Restaurant websites, hotel booking, and hospitality services. Menu display, reservation systems, location information, and visual food presentation.

### [Finance & Banking](./finance.md)
Banks, fintech apps, insurance, and investment platforms. Security signals, calculator tools, account dashboards, and regulatory compliance patterns.

### [Professional Services](./professional-services.md)
Law firms, consulting, agencies, and B2B services. Expertise showcasing, case studies, testimonials, and lead qualification.

### [Education & E-Learning](./education.md)
Universities, online courses, tutoring platforms, and EdTech. Course catalogs, enrollment flows, progress tracking, and credential display.

### [Local Business](./local-business.md)
Service businesses, retail stores, and local service providers. Location SEO, hours display, contact patterns, and review integration.

### [Media & Publishing](./media.md)
News sites, blogs, magazines, and content platforms. Article layouts, subscription models, ad integration, and content discovery.

### [B2B Enterprise](./b2b-enterprise.md)
Enterprise software, SaaS platforms, and business tools. Demo requests, pricing complexity, security badges, and integration showcases.

### [Nonprofit & Charities](./nonprofit.md)
Donation optimization, cause marketing, and impact storytelling. Trust signals, recurring giving programs, and donor retention patterns.

### [Crypto & Web3](./crypto-web3.md)
Web3 platforms, cryptocurrency services, and blockchain projects. Wallet connection UX, trust building in low-trust environments, and progressive disclosure.

### [Creator Economy](./creator-economy.md)
Content creators, membership platforms, and digital products. Subscription pricing, audience building, course pages, and multi-platform strategies.

## Quick Reference

### Universal Patterns (All Industries)

| Pattern | Why It Works | Source |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| Clear value proposition | Visitors decide in 3-5 seconds | [ContentGrip](https://www.contentgrip.com/website-first-impressions-5-seconds/) |
| Trust signals above fold | Reduces anxiety, increases engagement | — |
| Single primary CTA | Decision paralysis kills conversions | — |
| Mobile-first design | ~60% of traffic is mobile globally | [Statista](https://www.statista.com/statistics/277125/share-of-website-traffic-coming-from-mobile-devices/) |
| Fast load times | Each second delay = ~7% conversion drop | [Akamai via Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/learning/performance/more/website-performance-conversion-rates/)

### Industry Trust Signals

| Industry | Primary Trust Signals |
|----------|----------------------|
| E-Commerce | Reviews, secure checkout, return policy |
| Healthcare | Credentials, certifications, HIPAA compliance |
| Real Estate | Listings count, years in business, local expertise |
| Finance | Security badges, regulatory compliance, encryption |
| Professional Services | Case studies, client logos, credentials |
| Education | Accreditation, outcomes, instructor credentials |
| Nonprofit | Charity ratings, impact metrics, financial transparency |
| Crypto/Web3 | Security audits, team transparency, transaction previews |
| Creator Economy | Social proof, free samples, testimonials, subscriber counts |

### Industry-Specific CTAs

| Industry | Primary CTA | Secondary CTA |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| E-Commerce | "Add to Cart" | "Save for Later" |
| Healthcare | "Book Appointment" | "Call Now" |
| Real Estate | "Schedule Viewing" | "Get Pre-Approved" |
| Finance | "Get Quote" | "Learn More" |
| Professional | "Get Consultation" | "View Case Studies" |
| Education | "Enroll Now" | "Download Syllabus" |
| Nonprofit | "Donate Now" | "Learn About Our Impact" |
| Crypto/Web3 | "Connect Wallet" | "View Documentation" |
| Creator Economy | "Join Membership" | "Get Free Sample" |

## Research Methodology

All industry guides are based on analysis of:
- Top 10-20 performers in each vertical
- Industry benchmark reports (specific to each sector)
- User research and behavioral studies
- A/B test data from industry publications
