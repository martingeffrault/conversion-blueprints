## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Impact | Solution |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Too much jargon | -25% comprehension | Plain language, explain terms |
| No social proof | -35% trust | Customer logos, testimonials |
| Hidden pricing | +50% bounce | At least show tiers exist |
| Long forms | -50% completions | Progressive profiling |
| No clear CTA hierarchy | Confusion | One primary CTA per section |
| Feature-focused, not benefit | Lower engagement | Lead with outcomes |
| No demo/trial option | Lost opportunities | Multiple conversion paths |
| Poor mobile experience | -40% mobile leads | Mobile-optimized forms |
