### Donation Amount Best Practices

**Pre-set amounts:**
- Offer 4-5 preset amounts
- Anchor with mid-range amount selected by default
- Include custom amount option
- Show impact per amount ("$100 = 20 meals")

**Suggested amounts by nonprofit type:**

| Nonprofit Type | Low | Mid | High | Premium |
|----------------|-----|-----|------|---------|
| Food bank | $25 | $50 | $100 | $250 |
| Education | $35 | $75 | $150 | $500 |
| Medical | $50 | $100 | $250 | $1,000 |
| Environmental | $25 | $50 | $100 | $250 |
| Animal welfare | $20 | $40 | $80 | $150 |

### Monthly Giving Emphasis

> "Monthly gifts now make up 31% of online revenue and are rising steadily."

**Monthly giving benefits to communicate:**
- Sustainable, predictable impact
- Cancel anytime flexibility
- Exclusive monthly updates
- Special recognition

**Presentation:**
- Pre-select monthly option (62% of nonprofits prefer one-time, but monthly converts better)
- Show monthly donor count: "Join 2,400 monthly supporters"
- Highlight convenience: "Set it and forget it"

### Form Optimization

**Minimize fields:**
- Essential only: Email, Name, Card
- Optional: Address (only if tax receipts required)
- Remove: Phone, employer match, campaign code (unless critical)

**Mobile wallet integration:**
- Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal
- One-tap checkout increases mobile conversion significantly

**Cover fees option:**
- "Cover the $2.50 processing fee so 100% goes to our mission"
- 20-30% of donors opt in

## Mobile Optimization

### Mobile-Specific Requirements

> "53% of nonprofit traffic comes from mobile, yet mobile CVR is 27% lower than desktop."

**Critical optimizations:**
1. One-column donation form
2. Large touch targets (44px+)
3. Mobile wallet buttons prominent
4. Sticky "Donate" button
5. Minimal scrolling to complete

## SEO & Content Strategy

### Key Page Types

| Page | Purpose | SEO Focus |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Mission/About | Tell your story | Brand searches |
| Programs | Explain what you do | Service searches |
| Impact | Show results | "Best [cause] charity" |

### Content for Trust

- Annual reports (downloadable PDF)
- Financials page
- Leadership bios
- Beneficiary stories (with consent)
- News and press coverage
- Photo/video galleries

## Accessibility Requirements

> "Only 22% of nonprofit websites are designed for users with disabilities."

### WCAG Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Alt text on all images
- [ ] Keyboard navigation for forms
- [ ] Color contrast (4.5:1 minimum)
- [ ] Screen reader compatible
- [ ] Captions on videos
- [ ] Skip navigation links
- [ ] Accessible PDF alternatives
- [ ] Clear form labels

### Why It Matters

- Ethical obligation
- Legal requirements (ADA)
- Broader audience reach
- Foundation grant requirements
- Aligns with mission values

## Common Mistakes

### ‚ùå Hidden Donate Button

Donate CTA should be in header, visible on every page.

### ‚ùå Too Many Form Fields

Every field reduces completion. Ask only essentials.

### ‚ùå No Impact Communication

"We need money" vs "Your $50 feeds a family for a week"

### ‚ùå Generic Stock Photos

Real photos of real beneficiaries (with consent) convert better.

### ‚ùå No Mobile Optimization

53% of traffic is mobile. Test the experience.

### ‚ùå Outdated Information

Old news, past events, expired campaigns destroy trust.

### ‚ùå No Financial Transparency

Donors want to know where money goes.

## Nonprofit Website Checklist

### Homepage

- [ ] Mission clear in headline
- [ ] Impact statistics visible
- [ ] Donate button in header
- [ ] Beneficiary story/image
- [ ] Trust signals (ratings, seals)
- [ ] Email signup

### Donation Page

- [ ] Single-purpose page (no navigation distractions)
- [ ] Pre-set donation amounts with impact
- [ ] Monthly giving option prominent
- [ ] Minimal form fields
- [ ] Mobile wallet integration
- [ ] Security signals
- [ ] Cover fees option
- [ ] Mobile optimized

### Trust & Transparency

- [ ] Charity Navigator/GuideStar linked
- [ ] Program spending ratio displayed
- [ ] Annual report available
- [ ] Form 990 accessible
- [ ] Leadership bios
- [ ] Contact information clear

### Technical

- [ ] Load time <3 seconds
- [ ] Mobile responsive
- [ ] WCAG accessible
- [ ] SSL certificate
- [ ] Secure donation processor
