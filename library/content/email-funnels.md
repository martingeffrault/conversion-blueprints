## Universal Email Sequence Types

### 1. Welcome Sequence

First impression after opt-in. Sets the tone for the relationship.

**Timing:**
| Email | Delay | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Immediate | Welcome + deliver lead magnet |
| 2 | Day 3 | Discovery + brand story |
| 3 | Day 5 | Value + social proof |
| 4 | Day 7 | Soft pitch + next steps |
| 5 | Day 10 | Offer or engagement |

**Email 1: Welcome**
```
Subject: Welcome to [Brand] - Here's what you asked for

Hi [Name],

Welcome! I'm thrilled you're here.

As promised, here's your [lead magnet]:
[DOWNLOAD BUTTON]

Quick intro: [1-2 sentences about who you are and what you do]

Over the next few days, I'll share:
• [Benefit 1]
• [Benefit 2]
• [Benefit 3]

Talk soon,
[Signature]

P.S. Have questions? Just hit reply.
```

**Email 2: Story + Discovery**
```
Subject: Why I started [Brand] (quick story)

Hi [Name],

Before I built [Brand], I struggled with [problem].

I tried [solution 1], [solution 2], [solution 3]...

None of them worked until I discovered [insight].

That's why I created [Brand]—to help people like you
[achieve outcome] without [pain point].

Quick question: What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?
Just hit reply—I read every response.

[Signature]
```

**Email 3: Value + Proof**
```
Subject: [Customer Name] went from [Before] to [After]

Hi [Name],

Meet [Customer Name].

[1-2 sentences about their situation before]

After [using product/service], they:
✓ [Result 1]
✓ [Result 2]
✓ [Result 3]

Here's what they said:
"[Testimonial quote]"

Want similar results? [CTA]

[Signature]
```

> "Welcome emails outperform promotions by 320%."
>
> *Source: [Klaviyo](https://www.klaviyo.com/blog/abandoned-cart-benchmarks)*

### 2. Abandoned Cart Sequence (E-commerce)

Recover 10-15% of abandoned carts.

> "Three-email sequences generated $24.9 million compared to $3.8 million from single emails."
>
> *Source: [Klaviyo](https://www.klaviyo.com/blog/abandoned-cart-benchmarks)*

**Timing:**
| Email | Delay | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | 1 hour | Reminder |
| 2 | 24 hours | Social proof |
| 3 | 72 hours | Incentive |

**Email 1: Reminder (1 hour)**
```
Subject: Did you forget something?

Hi [Name],

Looks like you left something behind:

[PRODUCT IMAGE + NAME + PRICE]

Your cart is saved—ready when you are.

[COMPLETE PURCHASE BUTTON]

Need help? Reply to this email.

[Signature]
```

**Email 2: Social Proof (24 hours)**
```
Subject: Others love this too

Hi [Name],

Still thinking about [Product]?

Here's what customers are saying:

★★★★★ "[Review 1]" - [Customer]
★★★★★ "[Review 2]" - [Customer]

Your item is still waiting:
[PRODUCT IMAGE]

[COMPLETE PURCHASE BUTTON]

[Signature]
```

**Email 3: Incentive (72 hours)**
```
Subject: [X]% off—just for you

Hi [Name],

Your [Product] is still in your cart.

To help you decide, here's [X]% off:
Use code: [CODE]

[PRODUCT IMAGE]
Was: [Original Price]
Now: [Sale Price]

[CLAIM YOUR DISCOUNT BUTTON]

Offer expires in 24 hours.

[Signature]
```

**Benchmark:**
- Top-performing cart abandonment flows generate $28.89 per recipient
- Average is $3.65 per recipient
- High-AOV categories: up to $75.66 per recipient

### 3. Post-Purchase Sequence

Turn buyers into repeat customers and advocates.

**Timing:**
| Email | Delay | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|

| 2 | Shipping | Tracking info |
| 3 | Delivery + 3 days | Check-in |
| 4 | 7 days | Review request |

[Signature]
```

### 4. Re-Engagement / Win-Back Sequence

Re-activate dormant subscribers.

**Trigger**: No opens/clicks for 60-90 days

**Timing:**
| Email | Delay | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Day 1 | Reconnect |
| 2 | Day 4 | Value reminder |
| 3 | Day 7 | Final offer |
| 4 | Day 14 | Sunset (remove if no engagement) |

**Email 1: Reconnect**
```
Subject: We miss you! Here's 20% off

Hi [Name],

It's been a while since we connected.

We've added some new things since your last visit:
• [Update 1]
• [Update 2]
• [Update 3]

To welcome you back: 20% off your next order.
Use code: WELCOME20

[SHOP NOW BUTTON]

Hope to see you soon,
[Signature]
```

**Email 3: Final Offer**
```
Subject: Should we stop emailing you?

Hi [Name],

We haven't heard from you in a while.

If you're no longer interested, we understand.

But before you go—one last offer:
[Special offer details]

[STAY SUBSCRIBED BUTTON]

If we don't hear from you, we'll stop emailing
to keep your inbox clean.

[Signature]
```

**Re-engagement Subject Lines That Work:**
- "We noticed you've been a little distant lately"
- "Don't leave a good thing behind"
- "We've missed you! Here's 15% OFF EVERYTHING"
- "Do you still want to hear from us?"
- "Are we on the same page?"

> *Source: [Automizy](https://automizy.com/blog/re-engagement-email-campaigns/)*

## SaaS Email Funnels

### Trial Onboarding Sequence

Get users to "aha moment" before trial expires.

**Timing (14-day trial):**
| Email | Day | Purpose |
|-------|-----|---------|
| 1 | 0 | Welcome + quickstart |
| 2 | 1 | Key feature #1 |
| 3 | 3 | Social proof + feature #2 |
| 4 | 5 | Use case + feature #3 |
| 5 | 7 | Midpoint check-in |
| 6 | 10 | Value recap + upgrade hint |
| 7 | 12 | Final push + discount |
| 8 | 14 | Trial ending |

**Email 1: Welcome + Quickstart**
```
Subject: Your [Product] trial is ready

Hi [Name],

Welcome to [Product]! Your 14-day trial starts now.

Here's how to get started in 5 minutes:

Step 1: [Action + link]
Step 2: [Action + link]
Step 3: [Action + link]

[START NOW BUTTON]

Need help? Reply to this email or [book a call].

[Signature]
```

**Email 5: Midpoint Check-in**
```
Subject: Day 7: How's it going?

Hi [Name],

You're halfway through your trial.

Quick pulse check:

Have you tried [key feature]?
→ [Yes] / [Not yet]

Most successful users at this point have:
✓ [Action 1]
✓ [Action 2]
✓ [Action 3]

Where are you?

[CHECK MY PROGRESS BUTTON]

Need help? [Book a 15-min call]

[Signature]
```

**Email 8: Trial Ending**
```
Subject: Your trial ends tomorrow

Hi [Name],

Your [Product] trial ends in 24 hours.

Here's what you'll lose access to:
• [Feature 1]
• [Feature 2]
• [Feature 3]

Ready to continue?

[UPGRADE NOW BUTTON]

Questions? Reply or call [number].

[Signature]

P.S. Annual plans save 20%.
```

or [book a quick call].

```

For repeat customers.

## Subject Line Formulas

### High-Performing Patterns

| Pattern | Example | When to Use |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Question | "Still thinking about [Product]?" | Abandoned cart |
| Personalized | "[Name], your exclusive offer" | Promotions |
| Curiosity | "The one thing most people miss" | Nurture |
| Urgency | "24 hours left: [Offer]" | Limited time |
| Social proof | "Why 10,000+ chose [Product]" | Welcome/nurture |
| FOMO | "Don't miss what's new" | Announcements |
| Direct | "Your [Product] is ready" | Transactional |

### Subject Line Length

- **Under 50 characters**: Best for mobile
- **Under 30 characters**: Visible in full on most devices
- **Include emoji sparingly**: Can increase opens 10-20% (test first)

## Measurement Framework

### Key Metrics by Sequence

| Sequence | Primary Metric | Target |
|----------|---------------|--------|
| Welcome | Open rate | >50% |
| Abandoned cart | Recovery rate | 10-15% |
| Onboarding | Activation rate | Varies |
| Nurture | Click rate | >5% |
| Win-back | Re-engagement | >5% |

### Revenue Attribution

| Metric | Formula |
|--------|---------|
| Revenue per Recipient (RPR) | Total revenue / Emails sent |
| Revenue per Click (RPC) | Total revenue / Clicks |
| Flow Revenue | Sum of attributed purchases |
