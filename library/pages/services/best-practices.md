## Writing Services Copy

### Service Headlines

| âŒ Generic | âœ“ Benefit-Focused |
|------------|-------------------|
| Web Design Services | Websites That Convert Visitors to Customers |
| SEO Services | Get Found by Your Ideal Customers |
| Consulting | Solve [Specific Problem] in [Timeframe] |
| Marketing Services | Grow Your Revenue with Proven Marketing |

### Service Descriptions

**Structure:**
1. What it is (brief)
2. Who it's for
3. What outcome they get
4. Key deliverables (bulleted)

**Example:**
> **Website Design & Development**
>
> For growing businesses ready to level up their digital presence. We design and build conversion-focused websites that turn visitors into leads and customers.
>
> - Strategic UX research and wireframing
> - Custom, on-brand visual design
> - Mobile-responsive development
> - SEO-optimized architecture
> - Content management training

### Problem-Solution Copy

**Formula:**
1. Name the pain (they feel understood)
2. Agitate (make it urgent)
3. Present solution (your service)
4. Show proof (results/testimonial)
5. Call to action (next step)
