# Waitlist & Coming Soon Pages

> **Purpose**: Build anticipation, capture leads, and validate demand before launch.
> **Key stat**: Top-performing waitlist pages achieve 25-85% conversion rates.
> **Use cases**: Product launches, SaaS pre-launch, early access campaigns.

---

## When to Use

### Waitlist Page

- New product launch
- SaaS beta testing
- Capacity-limited offering
- Building buzz before availability
- Validating product-market fit

### Coming Soon Page

- Business/product not yet ready
- Website under construction
- New feature announcement
- Market expansion

---

## Conversion Benchmarks

| Metric | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|---------|------|-----------|
| Waitlist signup rate | 5-10% | 15-25% | 40%+ |
| Coming soon capture | 3-5% | 8-15% | 20%+ |
| Referral participation | 5-10% | 15-25% | 40%+ |
| Email open rate (post-signup) | 30% | 45% | 60%+ |

> "Top-performing waitlist pages achieve 25-85% conversion rates by leveraging psychological triggers, technical optimization, and data-driven growth strategies."
>
> *Source: [Waitlister](https://waitlister.me/growth-hub/guides/waitlist-landing-page-optimization-guide)*

## Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Too Much Information

Your page has one job: capture intent.

**Fix:** Remove navigation, limit text, single CTA.

### 2. No Social Proof

Empty pages feel risky.

**Fix:** Add signup count (even "Join 100+ others").

### 3. Vague Value Proposition

"Something amazing is coming" tells nothing.

**Fix:** Specific benefits + who it's for.

### 4. Broken Mobile Experience

83% of visitors are on mobile.

**Fix:** Test on real devices, not just emulators.

### 5. No Follow-Up

Capturing email but going silent kills momentum.

**Fix:** Automated welcome + regular updates.

### 6. No Referral Mechanism

Missing viral growth opportunity.

**Fix:** Add share buttons and referral rewards.

### Before Launch

- [ ] Clear, benefit-driven headline
- [ ] Single-field email capture above fold
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design
- [ ] Social proof (count, logos, or quote)

- [ ] Referral mechanism
- [ ] Welcome email (immediate)
