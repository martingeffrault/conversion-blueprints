# Content Hub Page Blueprints

> **Purpose**: Centralized content destinations that build authority, drive SEO, and capture leads
> **SEO value**: Keyword clusters, internal linking hubs, topical authority

---

## Available Blueprints

### [Glossary Pages](./glossary.md)

Definition pages that rank for "[term] definition" queries and serve as internal linking hubs.

**Benefits**:
- Rank for industry terminology queries
- Central hub for internal link distribution
- Build topical authority
- Attract natural backlinks

**Best for**: Industries with specialized terminology, SEO-driven content strategies

### [Resource Center Pages](./resource-center.md)

Comprehensive content libraries that house all content assets in an organized, searchable hub.

**Benefits**:
- Establish thought leadership
- Generate and nurture leads
- Improve content discoverability
- Enable sales team with shareable assets

**Best for**: B2B, SaaS, education-driven brands, enterprises

---

| Scenario | Glossary | Resource Center |

| Supporting customer education | ✅ | ✅ |

---

## Implementation Strategy

### Start with Glossary If:
- Your industry has 50+ terms users search for
- You need internal linking infrastructure
- You're building SEO foundation

### Start with Resource Center If:
- You have 20+ existing content assets
- Lead generation is primary goal
- You need organized content discovery

### Build Both If:
- Comprehensive content marketing strategy
- Multiple audience segments
- Long-term authority building goals
