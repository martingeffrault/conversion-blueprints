# E-commerce Homepage Blueprint

> **Purpose**: Showcase products, enable discovery, and drive visitors into the shopping funnel
> **Primary Goals**: Product discovery, category navigation, direct purchases
> **Typical Conversion Rate**: 1.88% average (Jan 2025), top performers 3-5%

---

## Core Principle

An e-commerce homepage is a storefront window and navigation hub. It must:
1. Immediately showcase compelling products
2. Make category navigation effortless
3. Build trust through social proof
4. Create urgency with promotions and limited offers

> "80% of users spend their time viewing directly above that page fold."
>
> *Source: [ConvertCart](https://www.convertcart.com/blog/high-converting-ecommerce-homepage)*

### Avoid Auto-Sliding Carousels

> "Swap out your hero-image auto-slide carousel."
>
> *Source: [GoInFlow](https://www.goinflow.com/blog/ecommerce-homepage-best-practices/)*

**Why**: Users often miss slides, conversion rates are lower, and it adds page weight.

**Alternative**: Static hero with clear CTA, or manual navigation controls.

## Urgency & Scarcity

> "Adding urgency, such as highlighting limited-time offers, can further boost conversion rates by 332%."
>
> *Source: [Brainspate](https://brainspate.com/blog/ecommerce-homepage-design-tips/)*

**Tactics**:
- Countdown timers for sales
- "Only X left in stock"
- "Selling fast"
- Limited-time offers in announcement bar
- Flash sale sections

---

## Performance

Speed directly impacts sales.

**Targets**:
- Page load: < 3 seconds
- LCP: < 2.5 seconds
- Mobile performance score: > 70

**Quick wins**:
- Lazy load images below fold
- Compress product images
- Use next-gen formats (WebP)
- Minimize third-party scripts
