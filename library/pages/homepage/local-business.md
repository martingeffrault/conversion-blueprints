# Local Business Homepage Blueprint

> **Business Type**: Location-based service or retail business
> **Primary Goals**: Drive calls, visits, and local leads
> **SEO Priority**: Very High (local search is primary traffic source)
> **Framework**: Trust + Accessibility

---

## Key Differences vs Other Homepages

| Aspect | Local Business | Agency | E-commerce |
|--------|----------------|--------|------------|
| Primary CTA | Call/Visit | Contact | Shop |
| Hero focus | Location + Service | Expertise | Products |
| Trust elements | Reviews + Local cues | Portfolio | Product reviews |
| SEO focus | Local pack, maps | Service keywords | Product keywords |

---

## Critical Local SEO Elements

### Must-Have On Homepage

| Element | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **NAP (Name, Address, Phone)** | Consistency for local SEO |
| **Service area** | Geographic targeting |
| **Click-to-call** | Mobile conversion |
| **Google Maps embed** | Location verification |
| **Reviews** | Local trust signals |
| **Local schema markup** | Rich results |

### NAP Consistency

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Business name | Exact match everywhere |
| Address | Formatted identically |
| Phone | Same number (local preferred) |
| Placement | Footer, Contact page, Header |

- Complete all fields
- Add photos regularly
- Respond to all reviews
- Post updates weekly
- Add services/products
- Enable messaging

## Trust Signals for Local Business

| Signal | Placement | Purpose |
|--------|-----------|---------|

| Years in business | About section | Longevity |
| "Locally owned" | Trust badges | Community connection |
| License numbers | Footer | Credibility |

## Industry-Specific Variations

### Restaurant/Food

- Menu prominently featured
- Reservation button
- Delivery options
- Food photos
- Health inspection info

### Healthcare/Medical

- Insurance accepted
- Provider credentials
- Appointment booking
- Telehealth options
- HIPAA compliance note

### Home Services (Plumber, HVAC, etc.)

- Emergency service highlighted
- Service guarantees
- Before/after photos
- Licensing info
- Free estimate CTA

### Retail Store

- Product categories
- Store locator
- Current promotions
- Online shopping option
- In-store pickup

### Professional Services (Lawyer, Accountant)

- Credentials/education
- Practice areas
- Consultation offer
- Client results
- Bar/CPA info

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| No phone number visible | Click-to-call in header + hero |
| Missing hours | Display prominently |
| No map/directions | Embed Google Map |
| Generic stock photos | Use real business photos |
| No reviews | Add Google/Yelp reviews |
| Inconsistent NAP | Standardize across all pages |
| No mobile optimization | Mobile-first design |
| Missing schema | Add LocalBusiness markup |
