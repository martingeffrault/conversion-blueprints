# Long-Form Sales Page

> **Purpose**: Persuade visitors to purchase a product, course, or service
> **Conversion Goal**: Purchase or booking
> **SEO Priority**: Low (traffic usually from email/ads)
> **Framework**: PASTOR or PAS extended

---

## When to Use

| Product Type | Best For |
|--------------|----------|
| Online courses | High-ticket education ($500+) |
| Coaching/consulting | Service packages |
| SaaS (high-touch) | Annual plans, enterprise |
| Info products | Ebooks, templates, toolkits |
| Events/conferences | Ticket sales |

---

## SEO Considerations

**Should you SEO-optimize sales pages?**

Generally **no**. Sales pages:
- Have traffic from owned channels (email, ads)
- Are conversion-optimized, not content-optimized
- Conflict between SEO structure and sales copy
- Often have thin navigation (intentionally)

**Exception**: Product-specific sales pages that target "[product name] review" or "[product] buy" keywords.


- Total value: $2,000+
- Today's price: $497

## Testimonial Best Practices

### What Makes a Great Testimonial

| Element | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **Specific result** | "I increased revenue by $15K" |
| **Before/after** | "Before I was struggling with... now..." |
| **Timeframe** | "Within 3 weeks" |
| **Objection handling** | "I was skeptical at first, but..." |
| **Relatability** | Similar to target audience |

### Testimonial Formats

1. **Text with photo** â€” Standard, credible
2. **Video testimonials** â€” Highest trust
3. **Screenshots** â€” Social proof, reviews
4. **Case study cards** â€” Before/after with stats

## Mobile Optimization

Sales pages are often 5,000+ words. Mobile considerations:

| Challenge | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| Long scroll | Sticky CTA button |
| Testimonial images | Stack vertically |
| Price comparison tables | Horizontal scroll or tabs |
| Video | Responsive embed |
| Forms | Full-width inputs |

## Page Length Guidelines

| Price Point | Recommended Length |
|-------------|-------------------|
| < $100 | Short-form (1,000-2,000 words) |
| $100-500 | Medium (2,000-4,000 words) |
| $500-2,000 | Long-form (4,000-7,000 words) |
| $2,000+ | Very long + sales call |

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Talking about features, not benefits | Translate features â†’ outcomes |
