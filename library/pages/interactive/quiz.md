# Quiz & Assessment Page Blueprint

> **Conversion benchmark**: 40.1% start-to-lead rate
> **Best for**: Lead generation, segmentation, product recommendations
> **Comparison**: 3-4x better than traditional lead magnets

---

## Why Quiz Funnels Work

Quiz funnels leverage several psychological principles:

1. **Curiosity gap**: People want to know their results
2. **Personalization**: Tailored outcomes feel valuable
3. **Micro-commitments**: Answering questions creates investment
4. **Reciprocity**: You give insights, they give contact info

> "Interactive quizzes perform 3-4 times better than traditional lead magnets. The overall conversion rate when people start a quiz is 40.1%."
>
> *Source: [Interact Blog](https://www.tryinteract.com/blog/quiz-conversion-rate-report/)*

## Best Practices

### Question Design

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Keep questions short (1-2 sentences) | Ask compound questions |
| Use conversational language | Use jargon or technical terms |
| Provide 3-5 answer options | Overwhelm with too many choices |
| Make all options appealing | Include obviously "wrong" answers |
| Use images for visual questions | Require long reading |

### Optimal Quiz Length

| Quiz Type | Questions | Completion Rate |
|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Personality | 7-10 | 75-80% |
| Assessment | 10-15 | 65-70% |
| Product finder | 5-7 | 80-85% |
| Knowledge | 10-12 | 70-75% |

### Lead Capture Placement

| Strategy | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| Gate before results | Highest capture rate | Some drop-off |
| Gate after teaser | Lower friction | Lower capture |
| Optional at end | Best UX | Lowest capture |

**Recommendation**: Gate before results with clear value preview

### Mobile Optimization

- Large touch targets for answer selection
- Progress bar visible at all times
- One question per screen
- Auto-advance after selection
- Easy back navigation

---

## Conversion Optimization

### Increase Start Rate

- Show time estimate ("2 minutes")
- Demonstrate value of results
- Use curiosity-driven headlines
- Show social proof numbers
- Include visual preview

### Increase Completion Rate

- Progress bar is essential
- Keep questions engaging
- Vary question types
- Save progress (for longer quizzes)
- Mobile-optimized interface

### Increase Lead Capture Rate

- Tease results before gate
- Show what's included
- Use trust signals (privacy, unsubscribe)
- Minimize form fields (email + first name max)
- Make value clear

### Increase Post-Quiz Conversion

- Personalized CTA based on results
- Clear next step recommendation
- Urgency where appropriate
- Social sharing options
- Follow-up email sequence

## Quizzes vs Calculators

| Factor | Quiz | Calculator |
|--------|------|------------|
| **Conversion rate** | 35-40% | 20-30% |
| **Lead quality** | Variable | High intent |
| **Best funnel stage** | Top of funnel | Bottom of funnel |
| **Engagement** | Higher (fun factor) | Lower (utility focus) |
| **Use case** | Awareness, segmentation | ROI proof, justification |

> "Quizzes are best for engaging, segmenting, and converting top-of-funnel traffic. Calculators excel at bottom-of-funnel lead qualification by providing personalized, actionable data."
>
> *Source: [Outgrow](https://outgrow.co/blog/quiz-vs-calculator-lead-generation/)*
