## Core Principle

A case study page must answer three questions:
1. **Who** is this customer? (Relatability)
2. **What** did they achieve? (Results)
3. **How** did your solution help? (Credibility)

> "The headline is arguably the most important element. Unlike a blog post, case study headlines should always focus on a specific result."
>
> *Source: [Edify Content](https://edifycontent.com/blog/b2b-case-studies-that-drive-revenue)*

### Headline Formula

Three required elements:
1. **Customer company name** â€” Shows it's real
2. **Product/service used** â€” Helps readers assess relevance
3. **Specific result** â€” The hook

**Examples**:
- "How Acme Corp reduced churn by 43% with ChurnBlock"
- "Spotify increases conversion 25% using Amplitude analytics"
- "How a 50-person startup saved 1,200 hours annually with Notion"

> "Stories, especially relatable ones, can alter our brain chemistry. Speak to the emotion of your buyer persona using the problems or pain points they care about."
>
> *Source: [LinkedIn - Donald Felix](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/b2b-case-study-template-15-headline-examples-donald-felix)*

Help readers determine if this customer is relatable to their situation.

### Elements to Include

| Element | Purpose | Example |

### Challenge Writing Tips

- Use specific numbers ("20 hours/week" not "too much time")
- Include a customer quote about the pain
- Quantify the cost of the problem
- Make it emotionally resonant

## Design Best Practices

### Visual Hierarchy

> "Bold Performance Metrics in the hero section immediately present key impact figures to capture attention."
>
> *Source: [Webstacks](https://www.webstacks.com/blog/b2b-case-study)*

**Priority order**:
1. Results/metrics (largest, boldest)
2. Customer name/logo
3. Headline
4. Customer quote
5. Body content

### Data Visualization

> "Using data visualization (charts, graphs, images, and infographics) improves engagement and makes important points more accessible."
>
> *Source: [Omniscient Digital](https://beomniscient.com/blog/writing-high-converting-b2b-case-studies/)*

- Before/after comparisons
- Bar charts for metric improvements
- Timeline graphics for implementation
- Screenshots of product in use

### Mobile Optimization

> "Mobile-friendly design is essential, as 63% of B2B buyers research on mobile."
>
> *Source: [Webstacks](https://www.webstacks.com/blog/b2b-case-study)*

- Stats stack vertically
- Quotes readable at mobile font sizes
- Video has prominent play button
- CTA buttons full-width on mobile

## Case Study Page Variations

### Short-Form (Quick Read)

For busy executives:
- Hero with 3 metrics
- 2-paragraph challenge
- 2-paragraph solution
- Results table
- Quote
- CTA

**Length**: ~500 words, 2-minute read

### Long-Form (Deep Dive)

For detailed research:
- All sections included
- Multiple quotes throughout
- Detailed timeline
- Technical implementation details
- Appendix with additional data

**Length**: 1,500-2,500 words, 8-12 minute read

### Video-First

For visual learners:
- Video embed as hero
- Key points summarized below
- Transcript available
- CTA after video
