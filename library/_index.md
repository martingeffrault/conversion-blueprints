## What Is This Library?

This blueprints library is a collection of proven frameworks, patterns, and templates for designing websites that convert. It covers everything from page structure to psychological triggers, with data-backed guidance on what works and why.

### Who It's For

- **Founders & Marketers** building landing pages and websites
- **Designers** optimizing conversion flows
- **Developers** implementing CRO best practices
- **Agencies** creating client deliverables
- **Anyone** who wants their website to perform better

### How to Use It

1. **Start with your goal** — Find the relevant page type or section
2. **Use the blueprints** — Copy-paste proven structures
3. **Apply the data** — Benchmark against industry standards
4. **Test and iterate** — Use the testing guides to optimize

## Philosophy

### Evidence-Based

Every recommendation in this library is backed by:
- Academic research and studies
- Industry reports and benchmarks
- Real-world case studies
- Established UX/CRO frameworks

### Practical First

These aren't theoretical concepts—they're actionable blueprints you can implement immediately. Each guide includes:
- Clear structures and templates
- Copy-paste examples
- Checklists for implementation
- Testing ideas for optimization

### Always Evolving

The web changes constantly. This library is designed to be updated as:
- New research emerges
- Best practices evolve
- Industry benchmarks shift
- New patterns prove effective
