# Call-to-Action (CTA) Best Practices

> **Purpose**: Drive visitors to take the desired action
> **Position**: Throughout pages, especially above fold and at section ends
> **Goal**: Maximize click-through and conversion rates

---

## Core Principles

The CTA is where all your page work pays off. A great page with a weak CTA fails. A mediocre page with a great CTA can still convert.

### The 3 CTA Requirements

1. **Visibility** â€” Can visitors see it immediately?
2. **Clarity** â€” Do they know what happens when they click?
3. **Motivation** â€” Is there a reason to click now?

> "CTAs with action-oriented language (Get, Start, Try) convert up to 90% better than passive language (Submit, Continue)."
>
> *Source: CRO industry studies*

## CTA Copy

### Action Verbs That Convert

| High-Performing | Low-Performing |
|-----------------|----------------|
| **Get** your free guide | Download |
| **Start** your trial | Submit |
| **Try** it free | Sign up |
| **Claim** your spot | Register |
| **Join** 10,000+ users | Click here |
| **Unlock** premium features | Buy |
| **See** how it works | Learn more |
| **Book** your demo | Contact |

### CTA Formulas

**Formula 1: Action + Benefit**
- "Get My Free Report"
- "Start Saving Time"
- "Unlock Premium Features"

**Formula 2: Action + Object**
- "Download the Guide"
- "Start Your Trial"
- "Book a Demo"

**Formula 3: Action + Urgency**
- "Claim Your Spot Now"
- "Get Started Today"
- "Try Free for 14 Days"

**Formula 4: First Person**
- "Start My Free Trial"
- "Get My Quote"
- "Yes, I Want This"

> "First-person CTAs ('Start my trial') can outperform second-person ('Start your trial') by 90% in some contexts."
>
> *Source: Unbounce research*

## A/B Testing Ideas

### Copy Tests

- First person vs. second person ("My" vs. "Your")
- Action verb variations (Get vs. Start vs. Try)
- With vs. without urgency
- Short vs. detailed (2 words vs. 5 words)

### Design Tests

- Button color
- Button size
- Rounded vs. sharp corners
- With vs. without icon

### Placement Tests

- Above fold position
- Sticky vs. non-sticky
- Number of CTAs per page
- Primary/secondary arrangement

## CTA Checklist
