# Analytics & Tracking Library

> **Purpose**: Measure what matters to make data-driven decisions
> **Usage**: Reference during planning, implementation, and optimization phases
> **Principle**: Measure outcomes, not vanity metrics

## Available Guides

### [Analytics Setup](./analytics-setup.md)
Comprehensive guide to implementing web analytics. Covers Google Analytics 4, tracking plans, event taxonomy, conversion tracking, and attribution models.

### [Tracking Implementation](./tracking-implementation.md)
Technical implementation patterns. Covers Google Tag Manager, data layers, e-commerce tracking, custom events, and debugging techniques.

Key performance indicators for different business types. Covers SaaS, e-commerce, lead generation, content/media, and marketplace metrics.

## Quick Reference

### The Measurement Framework

```
Business Objectives
       ↓
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
       ↓
Metrics & Dimensions
       ↓
Implementation (Tags, Events)
       ↓
Reporting & Action
```

### Universal KPIs

| Category | Metrics |
|----------|---------|
| **Acquisition** | Sessions, users, traffic sources |
| **Behavior** | Bounce rate, pages/session, time on site |
| **Conversion** | Conversion rate, goal completions, revenue |

### The Golden Rule

> "If you can't measure it, you can't improve it."
>
> *— Peter Drucker*
