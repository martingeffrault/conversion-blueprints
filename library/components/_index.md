## Available Patterns

### [Forms](./forms.md)
Form design for maximum completion rates. Covers field optimization, layouts (single column, multi-step), input types, validation, mobile optimization, and trust elements.

### [Navigation](./navigation.md)
Navigation patterns including headers, mega menus, mobile navigation, breadcrumbs, pagination, and footer structures. Accessibility-focused.

### [Cards](./cards.md)
Card-based interface patterns. Content cards, product cards, feature cards, pricing cards, testimonials. Includes layouts, sizing, and responsive behavior.

### [Trust Elements](./trust-elements.md)
Credibility signals: social proof, testimonials, logos, ratings, badges, guarantees, press mentions. Placement strategies by funnel stage.
